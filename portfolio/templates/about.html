About Page

Welcome to our financial portfolio management application!

Features

  • Track your investments and portfolio performance.
  • View real-time stock prices and historical data.
  • Manage your cash balance and transaction history.

How It Works

Our application provides a user-friendly interface to help you manage your financial portfolio efficiently. When you sign up, you'll start with a demo trading balance of $15,000. This allows you to explore the features of our platform without using real money.

Simply log in to your account, where you'll have the freedom to:

  • Explore real-time stock prices and historical data.
  • Track your investments and monitor portfolio performance.
  • Execute demo trades to simulate buying and selling stocks.
  • View detailed transaction history to analyze your trading decisions.

Our goal is to provide you with a risk-free environment where you can practice and refine your investment strategies. Feel free to experiment with different stocks, observe market trends, and make informed decisions based on the wealth of real-time market data available.

Navigation Bar and Different Pages

Our application is designed with various pages to cater to different aspects of your financial journey. Here's a brief overview:

  • Home Page: The home page offers a comprehensive overview of the two primary stock market indexes: S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100. Gain insights into the performance and trends of these key indices to make informed investment decisions.
  • Portfolio Page: Dive into the details of your current investment portfolio. View information on individual stocks, their quantities, and current values.
  • New Transaction Page: Practice buying and selling stocks in a simulated environment. Use the user-friendly interface to execute trades without real financial risk.
  • Transaction History Page: Review a detailed history of your past transactions. Track every buy and sell action to analyze your trading decisions and performance over time.
  • Stock Search Bar: Utilize the search bar to explore and discover stocks. Get real-time information on stock prices, trends, and company details. The search bar empowers you to make well-informed investment choices.
  • Balance Page: Monitor your available cash balance. Gain insights into the amount of free money in your account.
  • About Page: You're currently on the about page! Learn more about the application, its purpose, and how each page contributes to your financial management journey.

Explore these pages to make the most of our financial portfolio management app.
